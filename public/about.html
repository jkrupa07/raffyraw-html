Trust Their Gut.
Why does dog food need a chemistry degree to understand? That's what we asked when we started Raffy Raw. The answer we kept finding was: it doesn't. Dogs have thrived on raw meat for thousands of years. Somewhere along the way, the pet food industry decided they knew better. They created highly processed foods with ingredients lists that read like science experiments. We went back to basics. Real, raw meat with ingredients you can actually recognise. No marketing gimmicks, no unnecessary additives, no pretending to be something we're not.
